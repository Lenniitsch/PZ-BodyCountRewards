description=[h2]Features[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]Stats UI:[/b] Three tabs: progress, reward history, and full trait catalog
description=[*] [b]Right-click context menu:[/b] Quick-check kills and distance to next milestone
description=[*] [b]Smart trait conflicts:[/b] Mutually exclusive traits auto-resolved
description=[*] [b]43 traits[/b] in the pool, every single one individually toggleable
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Sandbox Options[/h2]
description=All configurable in-game by the host. No file editing needed.
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]Kill threshold[/b] per milestone (2 to 10,000)
description=[*] [b]Progressive or linear[/b] scaling
description=[*] [b]Reward priority:[/b] Remove negatives first, add positives first, or coin flip
description=[*] [b]Grant Missed Opportunities:[/b] Install mid-game with thousands of kills? Get all owed rewards at once
description=[*] [b]Positive rewards, negative removal, or both.[/b] Your body count, your rules
description=[*] [b]Individual trait toggles:[/b] Enable or disable any of the 43 traits
description=[/list]
description=
description=Drop chances scale with trait cost: cheap traits (1-2 pts) drop often, expensive ones (7-8 pts) almost never. Check the Catalog tab for exact percentages.
description=
description=[h2]Trait Pool[/h2]
description=[b]21 positive traits[/b] you can earn, [b]22 negative traits[/b] that can be removed.
description=Full list with rarity and drop chances visible in the in-game Catalog tab.
description=[b]Not included:[/b] Traits that grant perk points (Athletic, Strong, Fit...), profession traits, mod traits.
description=[b]Trait conflicts[/b] are handled automatically. The Catalog tab shows why a trait is unavailable.

description=[h2]FAQ[/h2]
description=
description=[b]Q: Do I keep rewards if I remove the mod?[/b]
description=A: Yes. Traits are permanently safed to your character.
description=
description=[b]Q: Thousands of kills but only 1 reward?[/b]
description=A: Enable "Grant Missed Opportunities" in sandbox options and kill one more Z to trigger the update.
description=
description=[b]Q: Why can't I get trait XY?[/b]
description=A: Right click > BodyCountRewards > Catalog tab. It tells you exactly why.
description=
description=[b]Q: Linear vs Progressive?[/b]
description=A: Linear = same kills per milestone. Progressive = each one needs more kills. Scaling factor controls how steep it gets.
description=
description=[b]Q: Athletic/Strong/Fit?[/b]
description=A: Maybe. Those grant perk points, so they need extra work. If enough people want it, I'll look into it.
description=
description=[b]Q: Works with mod traits?[/b]
description=A: Not yet. Vanilla only.
description=
description=[b]Q: Switching from "Kill Milestones (Zombies)"?[/b]
description=A: Just swap subscriptions. Kill count lives on your character. Earned traits stay. Only reward history won't carry over.
