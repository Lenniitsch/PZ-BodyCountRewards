description=[b]Compatible with B42.19+ | SP and MP[/b]

description=The spiritual successor to Circuit's [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3540255691]Kill Milestones (Zombies) B42[/url], built from scratch for more features and full sandbox control. Thanks for the inspiration, Circuit!
description=On an older B42 version? Circuit's mod works for [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3340134879]B41[/url] and [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3540255691]B42[/url].

description=[h2]Features[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]Stats UI:[/b] Three tabs for progress tracking, full reward history, and a trait catalog with rarity and conflict info
description=[*] [b]Notification queue:[/b] Halo text with staggered display so rewards do not flood your screen at once
description=[*] [b]Right-click context menu:[/b] Quick-check kills and distance to next milestone. Toggleable in Mod Options.
description=[*] [b]Smart trait conflicts:[/b] Mutually exclusive traits are resolved automatically. The Catalog tab shows exactly why any trait is unavailable.
description=[*] [b]43 traits[/b] in the pool (21 earnable, 22 removable). Every single one individually toggleable in sandbox settings.
description=[*] [b]Third-party addon API:[/b] Other mods can register their own traits. See the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3745224257]I Am Not Your Mom[/url] addon for an example.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Sandbox Options[/h2]
description=All configurable in-game by the host. No file editing needed.
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]Kill threshold[/b] per milestone (2 to 10,000)
description=[*] [b]Linear or Progressive scaling[/b] with adjustable steepness (0.1x to 2.0x)
description=[*] [b]Reward priority:[/b] Remove negatives first, add positives first, or coin flip
description=[*] [b]Grant Missed Opportunities:[/b] Install mid-run with thousands of kills? Get all owed rewards at once.
description=[*] [b]Positive rewards, negative removal, or both[/b]
description=[*] [b]43 individual trait toggles:[/b] Enable or disable any trait in the pool
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Trait Pool[/h2]
description=[b]21 positive traits[/b] earnable, [b]22 negative traits[/b] removable. Full list with rarity tiers visible in the in-game Catalog tab.
description=[b]Not included:[/b] Traits that grant perk points (Athletic, Strong, Fit), profession-locked traits, or traits added by other mods unless registered via the addon API.
description=
description=[h2]Compatibility[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*] Build 42.19+ (Unstable). SP and MP.
description=[*] Safe to install mid-game. Reads your existing kill count.
description=[*] Safe to remove. Earned vanilla traits stay permanently on your character. Custom traits from third-party addons are the responsibility of their authors. If you remove an addon that registered non-vanilla traits, those traits may crash your save.
description=[*] No vanilla file overwrites. Works with Skill Recovery Journal.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]FAQ[/h2]
description=
description=[b]Q: Do I keep rewards if I remove the mod?[/b]
description=A: Yes. Earned vanilla traits are permanently saved to your character.
description=
description=[b]Q: Thousands of kills but only 1 reward?[/b]
description=A: Enable "Grant Missed Opportunities" in sandbox options and kill one more zombie to trigger the catch-up.
description=
description=[b]Q: Why can't I get trait XY?[/b]
description=A: Right click then Body Count Rewards, then the Catalog tab. It tells you exactly why. Conflicting trait owned, already earned, or toggled off in sandbox options.
description=
description=[b]Q: Linear vs Progressive?[/b]
description=A: Linear means the same kills per milestone. Progressive means each milestone needs more kills. The scaling factor controls how steep the curve gets.
description=
description=[b]Q: Works with mod traits?[/b]
description=A: Yes. BCR has a third-party addon API. Check out the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3745224257]I Am Not Your Mom[/url] addon for a working example, or build your own trait pack.
description=
description=[b]Q: Athletic / Strong / Fit?[/b]
description=A: Those grant perk points and need extra work. If enough people ask, I will look into it.
description=
description=[b]Q: Switching from Kill Milestones (Zombies)?[/b]
description=A: Just swap subscriptions. Kill count lives on your character and earned traits stay. Only reward history will not carry over.
