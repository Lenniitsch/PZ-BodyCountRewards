[ v1.3.0 — Body Count Rewards ]
We rebuilt the mod from the ground up for B42.19. Here's what changed:

[b]New Languages:[/b] We added auto-translated support for a whole bunch
of languages across both sandbox options and the stats UI. The translations
are machine-generated, so if something reads like a bad Google Translate
fever dream, let us know in the Workshop thread and we'll fix it.

[b]Third-Party Trait API:[/b] Other modders can now register their own
trait packs with BCR. Custom traits merge right into the reward pools,
stats UI, and history — no file overrides, no hacks. Check the
"I Am Not Your Mom" addon for a working example and template.

[b]Performance & Polish:[/b]
[list]
[*] Cached config and trait lookups — less per-tick overhead.
[*] Staggered notification queuing — no more halo text spam when
    you claim a batch of missed rewards.
[*] Stats UI rendering is smarter about when to redraw.
[/list]

[b]Bug Fixes:[/b]
[list]
[*] Fixed trait exclusion bugs where conflicting traits could be
    double-granted. You should no longer end up with both Adrenaline
    Junkie and Cowardly at the same time.
[*] Corrected several trait point costs to match vanilla PZ B42 values.
[/list]

[b]New Options:[/b]
[list]
[*] [b]Enable Debug Logging[/b] sandbox option — for when things go
    wrong and you need to send me a log file.
[*] [b]Show Context Menu[/b] toggle in the ESC menu (Mod Options) —
    hides the right-click menu entry if you're tired of seeing it.
    Not recommended, but it's your apocalypse.
[/list]

[b]Upgrading from v1.2.0:[/b] Your sandbox settings should carry over
fine. One option was renamed: "grand missed opportunities" is now
"grant missed opportunities" (I can spell now).
